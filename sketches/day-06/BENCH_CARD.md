# Manual Irrigation Bench Card

## LED Meaning

### LED ON

* Valve Energized
* Water Flow Allowed

### LED OFF

* Valve Closed
* No Irrigation

---

## Button Operation

### Press and Hold

* Valve Opens
* Irrigation Active

### Release Button

* Valve Closes
* Irrigation Stops

---

## Emergency Stop

Open the Serial Monitor.

Type:

```text
e
```

Press **Enter**.

### Result

* Valve OFF
* Relay OFF
* Irrigation Stopped

---

## Safety Reminder

Close the manual ball valve during:

* Maintenance
* Leaks
* Firmware faults
* Emergency situations
